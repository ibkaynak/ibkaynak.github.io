ibkaynak.com
ibkaynak.com is a student-built educational platform that fills the gap for IB students in Turkey who take niche School-Based Syllabus (SBS) subjects like Turkey in the 20th Century (TITC) and Turkish A: Literature. With limited resources available online for these subjects, the project was initiated to centralize all essential materials in one accessible place.

🌐 Website
ibkaynak.com

📚 Features
📄 TITC Past Papers (2013–2024): All available exams compiled in one archive

📝 Marked Turkish A Past Papers: Answered and scored for reference

🔬 IA and IO Assistance: Structured guides for Internal Assessments and Individual Orals

📊 Topical Resources: Original notes and categorized practice questions for Math AA, Physics, and Chemistry

🔗 Curated Links: External platforms for extended question practice and exam prep

👥 Audience
This project is designed specifically for:

IB students in Turkey studying SBS subjects

Students preparing for exams in TITC, Turkish A, Math AA, Physics, and Chemistry

Teachers looking for organized and up-to-date student-friendly resources

🤝 Contribution
At this stage, ibkaynak.com is a solo initiative, but contributions (notes, past papers, or improvements) are welcome. Contact via DM on Instagram.

📌 Motivation
The platform was born from the realization that many IB students in Turkey couldn’t access study materials for courses that aren’t globally mainstream. By creating ibkaynak.com, I aimed to empower local IB students with free, organized, and relevant content—all in one place.
